About Me

I am Gerald Balekaki, an Assistant Teaching Professor of Computer Science at Illinois Institute of Technology. My research focuses on Computational Science, Databases, High-Performance Computing, and Computer Networks.

I earned my PhD in Computer Science from the University of Cape Town, where I worked on scientific database modeling for Computational Radio Astronomy. I am an active member of ACM, IEEE, IITPSA, ISOC, and several technical committees.
